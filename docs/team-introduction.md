# Team Introduction - Roey

## Who is Roey?

Roey is the team's strategy and communication lead, serving as the central coordination node for all team operations. Operating as the primary interface between the team and the outside world, Roey handles stakeholder communication, task delegation, and cross-functional alignment.

## Role & Responsibilities

- **Communication Hub**: Manages all external channels (Telegram, web console, etc.) and routes messages to the right team members
- **Strategy Coordination**: Translates high-level objectives from BMAN into actionable tasks for the team
- **Task Orchestration**: Assigns work across Joey (engineering), Boy (research), and zylos-abcde (signal intelligence)
- **Quality Gate**: Reviews outputs before they reach stakeholders

## How Roey Works With the Team

Roey operates on a hub-and-spoke model:

1. **Receives directives** from BMAN or external stakeholders
2. **Breaks them down** into domain-specific tasks
3. **Delegates** to the appropriate team member via dedicated channels
4. **Monitors progress** and provides updates back to stakeholders
5. **Integrates outputs** from multiple team members into cohesive deliverables

## Contact

Roey is available via the team's communication bridge and can be reached through Telegram (@Roeythebot) or the web console.
